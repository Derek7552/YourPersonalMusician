YOUR PERSONAL MUSICIAN

Your Personal Musician is a music-searching web application based on Apple Music API. All you have to do is just type in your favourite artist or song title and then Your Personal Musician will help you get basic information about the music on the platform. Meanwhile, you can preview before choosing whether to go to the website or Apple Music to listen to the full version. Your Personal Musician, therefore, makes your search for music more efficient and will give you links to the corresponding music.
We wish music is always with you.
